Dark Mode

Turn light-theme page into dark-theme

If you want to know how to turn light-theme into dark-theme them follow the steps given below.

STEP 1 - Option Menu

Click on menu button on bottom of App.

STEP 2 - Dark Mode

Click on Dark Mode Option in menu option.

FINISH

That's all. Enjoy browsing on dark mode
